SOME THINGS ABOUT ME:

I was born in Karlsruhe, Germany and have been playing tennis since I was 5 years old. Now I am a proud member of the NJIT Men's tennis team. Currently I am a Junior studying Web and Information Systems in Newark. I am not a bad coder, but front end design is my true passion. On this website you will find some information concerning my education, interests, hobbies and ways to to contact me. Enjoy!

Experiences

Education

Before coming to the US I attended Walafrid-Strabo Gymnaisum in Germany. My grade average was a 2.5 which is comparable to a GPA of 3.2. Since Spring 2015 I have been attending the New Jersey Institute of Technology. I started out as a Digital Design student but quickly realized that it was not what I was looking for. For the last 4 semesters I have been majoring in Web and Information Systems. I love building and designing websites espacially when working with a group of people. I currently have a GPA of 3.61 and expect to graduate in Fall 2018.

Traveling

As part of my tennis career I got to travel to tournaments all over Europe. Switzerland, Italy, Denmark, Sweden and Bulgaria are only a few of the countries I got to visit. I fnished at a Junior world rank of 660. Attending these tournaments and taking a look at the all the different cities and cultures was one of the best parts of my life so far. I currently give tennis lessons in Germany, coaching kids and adults.

Art

Since a very young a age I always loved creating things. Until I was occupied with my tennis travels I was part of a band, playing hte guitar and singing. I also tried myself as a street artist and still draw on a regular basis. Berlin was huge inspiration, since the entire city is overdrawn by graffitties and stickers. I wouldn't consider myself an artist, but I look up to any one that is able to speak to the world through powerul images, no matter how small or big.
